Meet the Bride and Groom


Meet Jasmine

Born in St. Louis, Jasmine has been ambitiously charasimatic from a young age. She has always made it her goal to accomplish whatever she has decided to do, and has been successful thus far. She aspires to enter the field of forensic psychology where she can assist, mentally, the people who need it the most.


Meet Joshua

The Chicago born native with the lovable personality.... Joshua is a graduate of Roosevelt University class of 2013. He is currently pursuing a career in Information Technology. Despite his ever evolving fascination with gizmos and gadgets, his passion has always been loving people.


Jas & Josh


Jasmine and Joshua, now soon to be married, met at Roosevelt University during their freshmen year of college. More specifically, they were classmates in professor Monica Westin's English 102 class. In class, Jasmine was often annoyed at the amount of questions Joshua asked, and assigned him the moniker teacher's pet! Joshua believed Jasmine to be another black woman with an attitude, and mentally criticized a litter white skirt she wore occasionally. Despite previous dispositions, Joshua realized he had never actually talked to Jasmine, so he approached her after a lecture one afternoon. After a while of moderate dislike for Mr. Harrison, Jasmine began to grow fond of him. A conversation lead to sharing a meal, and not too long after, Jasmine and Joshua were inseparable. Beginning to date later that year, Jas and Josh dated nearly 4 years before he popped the question on July, 4th 2013. She, evidently, said yes! Now, they're Almost Harrisons!
